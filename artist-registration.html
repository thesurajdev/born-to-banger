Frequently Asked Questions

Is there any participation fee?

Our registration is completely free. We focus on creating opportunities for emerging and established artists alike.

Can I register in more than one category?

Absolutely! We encourage you to let us know if you work across multiple disciplines.

How do I receive confirmation after I register?

After you submit your registration via the Google Form, a confirmation email with additional details will be sent to your registered email address.

How long does the registration process take?

The registration process typically takes just a few minutes. Once submitted, our team will review your details and get back to you promptly.

How will my data be used?

Your information will only be used for the purpose of event coordination and artist selection. We value your privacy and never share your details with third parties without your consent.

Who can I contact if I have more questions?

If you have any additional questions or need further assistance, feel free to contact us at Born To Bangereventcoordinator@gmail.com and we will be happy to help.
